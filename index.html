Legal Link Confidential

Legal Link Confidential is a trusted job placement agency in Washington. With today’s highly competitive legal market, you can’t afford to make mistakes when it comes to employment or who you welcome into the company. From project attorneys and in-house counsel to legal support staff and even senior partners, the right people can make the biggest difference between your company simply existing in the market and competing and consistently reaping rewards. We at Legal Link Confidential fully understand this challenge. In the ever-shifting legal landscape, you can count on Legal Link Confidential to meet your unique needs and help your company grow. We hire only the best recruiters to find and place the best candidates in the right positions and the right companies. They completely understand that finding the perfect candidate for you is so much more than reading a couple of resumes which can be deceiving and isn’t based on titles that don’t always line up with experience. They know what to ask candidates to properly gauge their skill set. With ongoing training programs and vast corporate resources, we also expect our recruiters to bring quality and dedication to their work to be able to provide you only the best. More than that, we deliberately set the bar for our company and candidates high to remain true to our vision and mission. At Legal Link Confidential, we understand that our job can affect not just the company but also the candidate’s career. In addition to working on our recruiters, we also forge close relationships with both candidates and law firms. This allows us to fully determine what each firm is looking for in terms of employment and what they need. It also allows us to know more information about each candidate enough to discern which employers would be the best fit for them. In short, we get to know the goals of both the company and the candidate and guide them on how to get there and further their careers.
